MassMesh Public Hotspot

MassMesh is a community-owned and volunteer-run public wifi network. Enjoy your access to the network courtesy of a volunteer near you! Learn more about our project including how you can extend the mesh at MassMesh.org.

In connecting to this network, you agree to the following terms:

  • Use of the network is provided on an as-is basis
  • Do not use the network in connection with any illegal activity
  • Network resources are to be used reasonably - please refrain from disrupting the operation of this and other networks
  • Do not engage in activities that may harm other users of the network such as sniffing / intercepting traffic, introducing malware, or spoofing network protocols
